Lash Care

Aftercare essentials.

A little care goes a long way. Follow these to keep your set full, clean, and lasting.

Avoid getting your lashes wet for the first 24–48 hours.

Do not rub, pull, or pick at your lash extensions.

Clean your lashes daily using a lash-safe cleanser to remove oils, makeup, and debris.

Avoid oil-based makeup removers, cleansers, and skincare products around the eyes.

Brush your lashes gently each day with a clean spoolie.

Avoid sleeping face-down, as this can cause lashes to bend or fall out prematurely.

Do not use mascara on your lash extensions.

Avoid eyelash curlers, as they can damage both your natural lashes and extensions.

Schedule refill appointments every 2–3 weeks to maintain a full look.

Following these tips will help extend the life of your lash extensions and keep your natural lashes healthy.
